### KitchIN

Website: [Under Construction] (Coming Soon...)

Apple App Store: [KitchIN] (Coming Soon...)

Google Play Store: [KitchIN] (Coming Soon...)

GitHub: [joshualim30/kitchin](https://github.com/joshualim30/kitchin)

KitchIN is a mobile app that helps you and others you live with manage your kitchen. You can add items to your pantry, fridge, and freezer, and track what you have and what you need. You can also create shopping lists, meal plans, and recipes, and share them with others. The app is designed to help you save time and money, reduce food waste, and eat healthier. It will also feature an AI integration called Chef.ai, which will help you come up with recipes based on what you have in your kitchen. I built this app using Flutter and Firebase, and it is currently in development and will be released soon.
